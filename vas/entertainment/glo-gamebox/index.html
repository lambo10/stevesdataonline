Glo GameBox

Introducing an exciting mobile phone app that is bound to change the face of gaming in Nigeria, say hello to Glo GameBox, a mobile app that will move you to a whole new height of entertainment in mobile gaming. The service is available on both one-time and auto-renewal.

It contains all your favorite games including explosive and addictive series like Colin McRae Rally, Mini Metro, Ice Rage, Table Top and Cut the Rope.

The app has benefited from the rich expertise of the world’s leading games makers with more than 400 premium games from a wide variety of genres, including action, adventure, arcade, brain & puzzle, casual, kids, racing, sports and strategy.

All it takes to get in on all the fun is to SMS the word “GAMES” or “Games” to 900 (Toll Free), or dial *900# or *577#. The link to GameBox (http://glogamebox.applandstore.com/en/) is then sent to your phone.

Subscription cost:

Subscription cost for Glo GameBox is
• N15 for a day
• N55 for a week
• N155 for a whole month
• The service is available on both one-time and Auto-renewal mode.

All the categories offer you access to more than 400 premium games to download.
